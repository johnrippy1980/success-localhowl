The Challenge

A Growing Franchise Stuck on a Starter Website

NEUAGE Health + Wellness is a cutting-edge medical clinic specializing in hormone replacement therapy, medical weight loss, peptide therapy, and vitamin optimization. They had expanded to 6 locations across Missouri, Kansas, Florida, and Alabama, but their website told a different story.

Built on Duda, the old site was a JavaScript-rendered SPA with minimal content, no blog, no structured data, and a single-market positioning that said "Saint Louis, MO" while the business was operating in 4 states.

  • Duda SPA with JS-rendered content invisible to search engines
  • No structured data or schema markup for any location
  • Zero blog content for organic keyword capture
  • Single-market positioning despite multi-state operations
  • Inconsistent URL structures with typos (/francshise-opportunities)
  • No dedicated location landing pages for local SEO
  • Generic service descriptions with thin content
  • Missing online booking integration
The Solution

Built for Scale, Optimized for Every Market

We replaced the entire platform with a WordPress + Elementor build on WP Engine, designed to support NEUAGE's growth as a medical franchise while driving local patient acquisition in every market.

Platform Migration

From Duda SPA to WordPress + Elementor on WP Engine. NitroPack for performance optimization. WooCommerce for products. Real infrastructure for a real franchise.

Multi-Location SEO Architecture

Dedicated landing pages for every location with full LocalBusiness schema markup, unique content, and geo-targeted CTAs. Each market gets its own search presence.

Content Strategy

Active blog with topical authority content: FDA hormone therapy updates, peptide therapy research, immunity guides. Every post drives organic traffic and positions NEUAGE as the expert.

Service Page Depth

6 comprehensive service pages with detailed descriptions, treatment explanations, and conversion paths. From "Learn More" buttons to "Book Now" in one click.
