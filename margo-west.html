The Challenge

A 40-Year Legacy Deserved Better

Margo West has been a fixture in Dallas bridal couture since 1984. But their website told a different story. Built on a standard Divi template, it looked like every other bridal boutique online.

  • Generic bridal template that blended in with competitors
  • No clear service differentiation (alterations, custom gowns, preservation)
  • Missing founder story and 40-year heritage messaging
  • No online booking integration (phone-only)
  • Weak SEO structure with no schema markup
  • Slow page load times on mobile
The Solution

Design That Tells the Story

We rejected every bridal website cliche. No white backgrounds. No stock roses. No generic serif fonts. Instead, we built a site that feels like walking into a couture atelier.

Dark Over Light

Deep green and navy palette with gold typography. In a sea of white bridal websites, Margo West now stands out instantly. The dark palette signals luxury and sophistication.

Texture Over Text

Macro photography of actual couture beadwork replaces generic model shots. Visitors feel the craftsmanship before reading a single word.

Craft Over Commerce

"We do the alterations other designers won't" leads with capability, not inventory. Positions Margo West as a design studio, not a dress shop.

Heritage Over Hype

The founder's 40-year journey is front and center. Trust earned through decades of work, not marketing speak.
